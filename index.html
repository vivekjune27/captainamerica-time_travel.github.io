About the Game

After defeating Thanos once and for all in Avengers: Endgame captain Steve Rogers aka Captain America volunteer to return the Infinity Stones to their proper places in the timeline. While returning each stone he comes across whereas challenges which he might not be prepared for. To avoid conflict in multiple timelines he must deliver the stones on time.
